Privacy Policy

The Orange Filter

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

The Orange Filter is a browser extension that filters political content from your browsing experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No tracking of any kind

Local Processing

All filtering operations occur entirely on your device:

  • Text filtering: Performed locally in your browser
  • AI image analysis: Processed locally using TensorFlow.js - images never leave your device
  • Settings and preferences: Stored locally in your browser's storage

Permissions Explained

The extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • storage: Save your settings locally
  • activeTab: Determine which site you're viewing for whitelist functionality
  • scripting: Inject the content filter into web pages
  • declarativeNetRequest: Block URLs containing filtered keywords
  • offscreen: Run AI image analysis in an isolated worker

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or APIs. The AI model is bundled with the extension and runs entirely offline.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in extension updates.
