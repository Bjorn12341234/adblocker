The Orange Filter - Installation Manual
========================================

Thank you for downloading The Orange Filter! Follow these steps to install the extension in Google Chrome:

1. Extract the ZIP File:
   - Right-click the downloaded 'the-orange-filter-v1.zip' and select "Extract All" or use your preferred unzip tool.
   - You will now have a folder named 'dist'.

2. Open Chrome Extensions:
   - Open Google Chrome.
   - In the address bar, type: chrome://extensions
   - Press Enter.

3. Enable Developer Mode:
   - In the top right corner of the Extensions page, find the "Developer mode" toggle.
   - Switch it to ON.

4. Load the Extension:
   - Click the "Load unpacked" button that appeared in the top left.
   - In the file picker, navigate to and select the 'dist' folder you extracted in Step 1.
   - Click "Open" or "Select Folder".

5. Pin for Easy Access:
   - Click the Puzzle Piece icon (Extensions) in your Chrome toolbar.
   - Find "The Orange Filter" and click the Pin icon next to it.

6. Configure:
   - Click the orange icon in your toolbar to toggle the filter or access the Options page to customize your blocklist.

Enjoy a cleaner browsing experience!
